Videography
services

Valley Studios is a highly configurable space with many uses. As well as our recording studio, we also have the ability to provide videography services. Feel free to hire or bring your own equipment and just pay for the space, alternatively contact us with a list of your required equipment and we will sort out the pick up and return of all equipment.

Procurement fee for video sets starts at £20. If more than one trip is required this will be reflected in the price.
Check out our video tour on YouTube or call us to arrange a visit.

Prices for the use of the room start at £200 for 8 hours and discounts are available for booking larger time blocks. Contact us to get a price on your next project.

Our facilities are available for 24 hour use at weekends with load in starting from 6AM on Friday morning and load out finishing no later than 7:30AM Monday morning.

Need audio recording facilities? Valley Studios can offer a wide range of audio services for your next project. Out vocal booth is the perfect place for voice-overs and ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement). Our larger recording booth is ideal for Foley and any larger scale audio operations that might be needed. Check out our recording studio and see what we have to offer!

Have you filmed a project and need to compile it? Do you need to add or tweak the audio to your project or need a neutral common space to work with other parties? Our control room can house 4 nicely spaced duel screen work stations as well as our audio workstation. Check out our recording studio and feel free to Contact us to see what Valley Studios has to offer!
